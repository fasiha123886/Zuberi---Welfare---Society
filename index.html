Who We Are

Bringing Hope and Dignity to Families

Founded in Aligarh, Uttar Pradesh, Zuberi Welfare Society is committed to creating sustainable community structures. We address local poverty, illiteracy, and lack of healthcare by working closely with community members on the ground.

Our focus is on empowering the youth and women, building local capacity, and acting quickly to assist during seasons of high medical or emergency needs.

Our Vision

An inclusive society where basic health, education, and livelihood are accessible to every individual without disparity.

Our Mission

To provide immediate educational aid, vocational independence to women, health diagnostics, and relief to slums in Aligarh.

Mr. Asmat Ali Khan Zuberi

Founder & President

Mr. Asmat Ali Khan Zuberi has been doing social work across Uttar Pradesh since 1970. He donated his own land in Islamnagar, Badaun to build a masjid and madarsa, and later donated 2 beegha of land to help found Zuberi Welfare Society.

What We Do to Help

We approach community development holistically, focusing on long-term sustainability alongside immediate relief support.

Health Camps

Free diagnostic checkups, general physical checks, vision screenings, and free medication distributions in slums.

Education Support

Free remedial tuition centers, educational stationery support, books distribution, and school scholarships.

Women Empowerment

Relief & Aid
